ABOUT US

About Global Tour, our goals and objectives and who is behind it.

What we do

Global Tour is one of the web's largest and most active travel communities with members representing every country in the world. If you're thinking about travelling soon or ready to plan your trip - Global Tour is where your journey begins.

Our Goals

"The goal of Global Tour is to create an international meeting point for travellers worldwide, whether they are planning their travels, currently travelling or have returned from their travels and want to stay in touch with (or find) those travel friends they met while travelling in the past."

Our Objective

"Global Tour's objective is to enrich the travelling experience by offering numerous services, content and products that have significant perceived and added value for travellers worldwide. Global Tour will create more understanding for different cultures and countries and ultimately a better world for everyone to travel in."
