  • Getting Started
    • Creating and accessing your account

      If you already have an Hellotravel premium account, you can be up and running on Pay with HelloTravel in no time. Just complete the KYC process and you will be ready to accept payments from the buyers.

    • What do I need for KYC?

      KYC is a simple process to authenticate a seller on PayWithHelloTravel

      It requires verifcation of your:
      1. PAN number
      2. Account number
      3. IFSC code

    • Pay with IndiaMART Pricing

      A fee of only 1.75% per transaction will be levied on all modes of payments (including Credit/Debit cards, Net-banking, Wallets, ATM-cum- debit). The fees displayed is excluding taxes. We will apply GST of 18% to the fees displayed above.

      For Example:
      On a transaction of Rs. 10,000;
      The fees applied will be 1.75% of 10,000 = Rs. 175
      +
      GST (18% of 175) = Rs. 31.5
      Net payable to seller = 10,000 - 206.5 = Rs. 9793.50

    • Buyer journey on Pay with IndiaMART

      Pay with HelloTravel works on your Hello Travel catalog as follows:

      1. Buyer goes to your HelloTravel catalog and clicks on Pay with HelloTravel
      2. Enters his/her mobile number and product details
      3. Use Pay with HelloTravel payment methods to make the payment
      4. After a buyer has done the payment, you'll get a notification to accept the payment
      5. If you are not a pre-approved KYC supplier, you'll have to verify yourself through our KYC process. Then the payment will be released into your bank account.

    Managing Payments
    • Do I have access to my payments?

      Yes, you can go through the payments you have received in the tab "Payments Received" on your dashboard. You can also download the invoices using "Download Receipts" tab or "Download Receipt" inside the description of every invoice.

      Where can I see My Invoices?

      Open "My invoices" tab on seller dashboard to find a list of all the 'Paid' or 'Pending' invoices.You can also download the invoices using "Download Invoices" tab or "Download Invoice" inside the description of every invoice.

      Are My Payouts recorded?

      To check your payouts please check My Payouts screen on your dashboard. You can also find the Net Amount paid by Pay with IndiaMART and the respective tax breakup for every payout sent.
