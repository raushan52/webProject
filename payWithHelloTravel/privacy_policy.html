Provided that you agree to the usage of cookies on your computer, the latter can provide valuable analysis about the sites that you visit regularly and other important web traffic information. Basically, cookies are used by any website to remember the browsing behaviour and preferences of users. PAYWITHHELLOTRAVEL may also use Web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags or Web bugs), which are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, that are placed in the code of a Web page. Users are hereby notified that such cookies are used only for statistical purposes and are removed as soon as their purpose is served. Of course, if you disable cookies on your system, you cannot avail of this benefit.
